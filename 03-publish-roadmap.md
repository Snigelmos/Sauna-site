## Publishing guidance

Publish 2 articles per week. Start with commercial pages because they define the site architecture and affiliate model. Add informational health/recovery pages after the commercial pages exist so they have useful internal links.

Do not publish medical or detox pages quickly. They need stronger sourcing and more careful claims.
