# Sauna site research dossiers

Citation-grade source material gathered for content + tools. Each file lists the claim, the number, and the source URL so pages can cite responsibly. All figures are US, 2026, and are planning ranges, not quotes.

## Files
- `cost-calculator-model.md` - data model + cost ranges + formula for the `/tools/sauna-cost-calculator/` homemade-sauna estimator (the flagship tool).
- `sauna-health-benefits.md` - evidence base for the sauna-benefits post (peer-reviewed + Mayo/Harvard/Cleveland Clinic). Conservative-claim guidance included.
- `red-light-therapy.md` - evidence + device parameters for the red light therapy adjacency guide.
- `sauna-buying-specs.md` - sauna types, temperatures, heat-up, electrical, EMF, wood materials, and sizing. Feeds `best-home-sauna`, `infrared-vs-traditional-sauna`, and product cards.

## E-E-A-T rules these dossiers must enforce on-page
- No fabricated testing, ownership, ratings, or expert review.
- Health claims stay conservative; cite primary/authoritative sources; never promise treatment, cure, detox, weight loss, or disease outcomes.
- Costs are presented as ranges with visible assumptions and an "estimate only" disclaimer.
- Encourage professional advice (electrician for wiring, physician for at-risk health users).

## Source quality note
Peer-reviewed and major-clinic sources (JAMA, Mayo Clinic Proceedings, Harvard Health, Cleveland Clinic, Springer/Frontiers systematic reviews) are used for health claims. Cost/spec figures are synthesized from multiple 2026 industry pricing guides and cross-checked; they are directional planning ranges and must be labeled as such.
