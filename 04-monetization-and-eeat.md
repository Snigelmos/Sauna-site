# 04 - Monetization and E-E-A-T

## Monetization model

Primary revenue should come from direct affiliate programs and Amazon Associates where direct programs are unavailable.

Likely program categories:

- Home infrared sauna brands: Sun Home, Clearlight, Sunlighten, HigherDose.
- Outdoor/barrel sauna brands: Almost Heaven, Redwood Outdoors, Dundalk LeisureCraft, Backyard Discovery.
- Portable and blanket products: HigherDose, MiHigh, LifePro, Therasage, SereneLife.
- DIY sauna suppliers: Harvia, Finnleo, Superior Saunas, MySaunaWorld, SaunaPlace.
- Red light therapy: Joovv, Mito Red Light, PlatinumLED, HigherDose.
- Cold plunge later: Plunge, Renu Therapy, Ice Barrel, Sun Home.

## Affiliate rules

- Add a clear affiliate disclosure near the top of commercial pages.
- Mark paid/affiliate links with `rel="sponsored nofollow"` where supported.
- Do not place affiliate CTAs before useful comparison content.
- Explain who each product is best for, who should avoid it, and the tradeoffs.
- Never present sponsored placements as editorial recommendations.

## E-E-A-T requirements

For every commercial review or roundup:

- State the selection criteria.
- State whether products were tested, researched, or selected from specs and owner feedback.
- If not tested, do not use first-person testing language.
- Include visible last-updated dates.
- Include limitations and installation caveats.

For health/recovery topics:

- Use conservative claims.
- Cite authoritative sources for cardiovascular, recovery, heat exposure, red light therapy, detox, weight loss, and inflammation claims.
- Avoid promising treatment, cure, fat loss, detoxification, or disease outcomes.
- Encourage professional medical advice for users with cardiovascular risk, pregnancy, heat intolerance, medications, or other relevant health concerns.

## Red light therapy caution

Red light therapy can be monetizable, but the evidence and device claims are easy to overstate. Keep red light pages practical: device types, power/irradiance claims, treatment area, session time, safety, return policies, and realistic expectations.
